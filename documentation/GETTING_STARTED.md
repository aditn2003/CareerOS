# Getting Started Guide

Welcome to **CareerOS ATS** — your personal job search companion! This guide will help you get up and running in minutes.

---

## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to the CareerOS website
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"**
3. Choose one of these options:
   - **Email/Password**: Enter your email and create a password
   - **Google Sign-In**: Click "Continue with Google" for quick signup
4. Verify your email (check spam folder if needed)
5. You're in! 🎉

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. Navigate to **Profile** from the sidebar
2. Fill in your basic information:
   - Full Name
   - Phone Number
   - Location
   - LinkedIn URL (optional)
3. Click **Save** to update your profile

### Step 3: Upload Your Resume

1. Go to **Resumes** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Upload Resume"**
3. Select your PDF or DOCX file
4. Your resume is now stored and ready for AI tailoring!

### Step 4: Add Your First Job

1. Click **"+ Add Job"** from the Dashboard or Jobs page
2. Fill in the job details:
   - **Company Name**: e.g., "Google"
   - **Position**: e.g., "Software Engineer"
   - **Job URL**: Link to the job posting
   - **Status**: Select "Wishlist" or "Applied"
3. Click **Save**

Congratulations! You've set up your CareerOS account! 🚀

## Core Features

### 📋 Job Tracking

Keep all your job applications organized in one place.

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Wishlist** | Jobs you're interested in |
| **Applied** | Applications submitted |
| **Interview** | Got an interview! |
| **Offer** | Received an offer |
| **Rejected** | Application declined |

**Tips:**
- Use the search bar to find specific jobs
- Filter by status to focus on active applications
- Add notes to remember key details about each job

---

### 📄 AI Resume Tailoring

Let AI customize your resume for each job posting.

**How to Use:**
1. Go to a job's detail page
2. Click **"Tailor Resume"**
3. Select your base resume
4. AI analyzes the job description and suggests improvements
5. Review and download your tailored resume

**Best Practices:**
- Keep your base resume comprehensive
- Review AI suggestions — don't blindly accept all changes
- Save tailored versions for each company

---

### ✉️ AI Cover Letter Generator

Create personalized cover letters in seconds.

**How to Use:**
1. Go to a job's detail page
2. Click **"Generate Cover Letter"**
3. AI creates a cover letter matching the job requirements
4. Edit and personalize as needed
5. Copy or download

**Tips:**
- Always add a personal touch after AI generation
- Mention specific projects or experiences
- Keep it under one page

---

### 🎤 Interview Preparation

Practice with AI-powered mock interviews.

**How to Use:**
1. Go to **Interview Prep** from the sidebar
2. Select the job you're preparing for
3. Choose interview type:
   - **Behavioral**: STAR method questions
   - **Technical**: Role-specific questions
4. Answer questions and get AI feedback
5. Review your responses and improve

---

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard

Track your job search progress with visual insights.

**Metrics Available:**
- Applications by status
- Response rate
- Applications over time
- Top companies applied to

**How to Access:**
1. Click **Dashboard** in the sidebar
2. View your stats at a glance
3. Use insights to adjust your strategy

---

### 👥 Networking Contacts

Manage professional connections related to your job search.

**How to Use:**
1. Go to **Networking** in the sidebar
2. Click **"+ Add Contact"**
3. Enter contact details:
   - Name, Company, Role
   - Email, LinkedIn
   - How you met
   - Notes
4. Track follow-ups and conversations
