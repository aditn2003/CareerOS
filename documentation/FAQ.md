# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

## Account & Login

### How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" on the homepage. You can register with:
- **Email and password** — Enter your details and verify your email
- **Google Sign-In** — One-click signup with your Google account

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for a reset link (check spam folder too)
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Can I change my email address?

Currently, email addresses cannot be changed after registration. If you need to use a different email, please create a new account.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact us at **support@atscareeros.com** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and we'll process your request within 7 business days.

## Job Tracking

### How many jobs can I track?

There's no limit! Track as many job applications as you need.

### Can I import jobs from other platforms?

Currently, jobs must be added manually. Copy-paste the job URL and details from job boards like LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor.

### What do the job statuses mean?

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Wishlist** | Jobs you're interested in but haven't applied to yet |
| **Applied** | You've submitted an application |
| **Interview** | You've been invited to interview |
| **Offer** | You've received a job offer |
| **Rejected** | The application was declined |

### Can I add custom job statuses?

Not at this time. The current status options cover the standard job application workflow.

### How do I delete a job?

1. Open the job details page
2. Click the **delete/trash** icon or button
3. Confirm the deletion

## Resume Features

### What file formats are supported for resume uploads?

- **PDF** (recommended)
- **DOCX** (Microsoft Word)

### Is there a file size limit for resumes?

Yes, the maximum file size is **5 MB** per resume.

### How does AI resume tailoring work?

1. Our AI reads the job description you're applying for
2. It analyzes your uploaded resume
3. It suggests changes to better match keywords and requirements
4. You review, edit, and download the tailored version

### Will the AI change my personal information?

No. The AI only suggests changes to skills, experience descriptions, and formatting. Your contact info and work history remain accurate.

### Can I download my tailored resume?

Yes! After the AI generates suggestions, you can download the tailored resume as a PDF or DOCX file.

## Cover Letters

### How does the AI cover letter generator work?

The AI reads the job posting and your profile to create a personalized cover letter. It includes:
- Why you're interested in the role
- Relevant skills and experience
- Company-specific language

### Should I edit the AI-generated cover letter?

**Yes!** Always add a personal touch. Mention specific projects, use your own voice, and ensure accuracy.

### Can I save multiple cover letters?

Yes, each cover letter is saved with its associated job application.

Your responses are saved so you can review and improve over time.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data secure?

Yes. We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **HTTPS encryption** on all connections
- **Password hashing** with bcrypt
- **JWT authentication** for secure sessions
- **Rate limiting** to prevent attacks

### Who can see my data?

Only you can see your data. Your job applications, resumes, and personal information are private.

### Do you sell my data?

**No.** We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

### Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored on secure servers in the United States, hosted on industry-leading cloud platforms (Supabase, Render, Vercel).

### How long do you keep my data?

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

## Technical Issues

### The site is loading slowly. What should I do?

1. **Check your internet connection**
2. **Clear browser cache**: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
3. **Try a different browser**: We recommend Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
4. **Disable browser extensions**: Some extensions can interfere

### I'm getting a "CORS error." What does that mean?

This is usually a temporary issue. Try:
1. Refreshing the page
2. Clearing your browser cache
3. If it persists, contact support

### The site shows a blank page. How do I fix it?

1. **Hard refresh**: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
2. **Clear browser cache and cookies**
3. **Try incognito/private mode**
4. **Update your browser** to the latest version

### I can't log in with Google. What should I do?

1. Make sure pop-ups are enabled for our site
2. Clear cookies for google.com
3. Try logging in with email/password if you've set one up
4. Contact support if the issue persists
